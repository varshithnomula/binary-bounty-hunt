Riddle Challenge

1.I am red, sometimes green, and I grow on a tree. I keep the doctor away, or so people say.

2. I have hands but no arms, a face but no eyes. I tell time, but I never lie.

3. The more you take from me, the bigger I get. What am I?

4. I speak without a mouth and hear without ears. I have no body, but I come alive with the wind.

5. Forward I am heavy, but backward I’m not. What am I?

6. I’m seen in the middle of March and April, I’m not in June. What letter am I?

7. I am a seven-letter word. My first two letters refer to a male. The first three letters can refer to a female. The first four letters refer to a great man. The entire word refers to a great woman. What am I?
